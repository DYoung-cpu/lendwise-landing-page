Support

Questions?

What types of loans does LendWise offer?

We offer Conventional, Jumbo, FHA, VA, DSCR, Bank Statement, Fix & Flip, First Time Homebuyer programs, and HELOCs. Our team will help you find the best fit.

How long does the loan process take?

With Odin AI, qualified borrowers can close in as little as 21 days. We keep you informed every step of the way.

What is Odin AI?

Odin is our AI platform that provides instant answers, document analysis, and smart recommendations to make your mortgage experience faster and smoother.

What documents do I need?

Generally: pay stubs, W-2s, bank statements, tax returns, and ID. Odin will guide you through exactly what's needed for your loan type.

How do I get started?

Click "Apply Now" to begin your application online, or contact us and a loan officer will guide you through the process.
